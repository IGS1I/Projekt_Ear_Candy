Prototype Library Rules

1. The app will only support .mp3 files.
2. All music will be located inside a root folder on the SD card called /Music.
3. The app will only scan inside /Music (not the entire SD card).
4. Every .mp3 file found in /Music or its first-level subfolders is treated as a song.
5. Metadata such as title, artist, and album will be read from MP3 tags.
6. Artists and albums are generated from metadata, not folder names.
7. Every first-level folder inside /Music is treated as a playlist.
8. Songs placed directly inside /Music are part of the Songs library but not part of any playlist.
9. Nested folders inside playlist folders are ignored for the prototype.
10. Unsupported files are ignored.

Process

python3 scanner.py
pip3 install mutagen
pip3 install PySide6


methods 
scan_music_library() finds songs and folder-playlists
build_artists_library() creates the artist view data
build_albums_library() creates the album view data
